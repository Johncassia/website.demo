About Me

Meet Johnson, a creative and tech-savvy individual who combines his passions for website development, graphic design, and vintage typewriters. By day, he builds sleek and functional websites for clients using his coding skills and knowledge of modern design principles. By night, he transforms into a graphic design enthusiast, crafting beautiful visuals and logos using his trusty typewriters and a dash of creativity. Johnson's love affair with typewriters began when he stumbled upon an old Remington Noiseless Portable in a thrift store. Enchanted by its tactile keys and mechanical rhythm, he began experimenting with typographic art. Soon, he incorporated typewriters into his graphic design workflow, using them to create unique textures, patterns, and typography.
